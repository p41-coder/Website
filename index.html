Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a reservation?

To cancel a reservation, go to the "My Bookings" section on our website or contact our support team directly for assistance.

Are there any rooms available?

You can check room availability on the "Check Availability" page or contact us for more information.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and online payment options. Cash payments are accepted at check-in.

How can I use coupon codes?

Enter your coupon code during the checkout process on the payment page to apply your discount.

What are the age requirements for booking?

Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation. Children must be accompanied by an adult during their stay.
